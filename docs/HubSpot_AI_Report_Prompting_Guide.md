## ğŸ› ï¸ Advanced Prompting Strategies

### 1. Use Internal Property Names
HubSpot's AI performs significantly better when you use internal property names (e.g., `hs_analytics_source`) rather than just labels (e.g., "Original Source"). 

*   **Good:** "Show me deals by source."
*   **Best:** "Create a bar chart of **deals** showing the **sum of amount** grouped by **hs_analytics_source**."

### 2. Define Precise Timeframes
Avoid vague terms like "recently." Use HubSpot-native timeframe logic.

*   **Relative:** "This quarter", "Last 90 days", "Year to date".
*   **Static:** "Between 2024-01-01 and 2024-03-31".

### 3. Specify Aggregation Logic
Be explicit about how the numbers should be calculated.

*   **Count:** "Number of records"
*   **Financial:** "Sum of amount", "Average ARR"
*   **Time-based:** "Average days to close"
